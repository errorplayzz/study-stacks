Open Letter

Hey there, fellow students and academic aficionados! Ever found yourself endlessly scrolling through endless PDFs, desperately searching for syllabi and study materials scattered across the internet? You're not alone, and we've got something that'll make you wonder why you ever put up with it in the first place.

Vision: Making Student Life Easier At STUDY STACKS, we understand the struggles students face when trying to access syllabi and study materials for their courses at Maharshi Dayanand University, Rohtak (MDU). We've been there, done that, and we decided it was high time to make student life a whole lot easier.

Our vision is simple: No more countless hours wasted scrolling through labyrinthine PDFs, no more hopping from one website to another in search of study materials . We envisioned a world where students could access all their academic needs in one place, hassle-free.

Goal: Covering All MDU Courses Our goal is as straightforward as it gets: We want to cover all courses offered by MDU. From B.Tech to BCA, from the mainstream to the niche, we're on a mission to provide comprehensive coverage of the entire MDU curriculum.

We want every student, regardless of their program or semester, to find what they need at STUDY STACKS. We believe in the power of open-source collaboration, and that's why we're calling on developers, academics, and students alike to join our cause. Together, we can build a repository of academic resources that'll make student life easier, fun, and more productive.

So, if you've ever been a "noob" scrolling through endless syllabus PDFs, we've got your back. Let's embark on this journey to make academic life simpler, more accessible, and just a bit more fun.

Join us at STUDY STACKS , because academic navigation shouldn't be rocket science. It should be as simple as saying, "STUDY STACKS, help me out!"
